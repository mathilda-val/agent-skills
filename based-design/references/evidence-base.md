## THE SERIF/SANS-SERIF DECISION TREE

This is not about preference. It is about structural function.

| Choice | Structural Function |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Serif body text** | Signals literary/editorial register. Horizontal stress aids long-form reading. Creates "warm" grey texture. |
| **Sans-serif body text** | Signals contemporary/institutional register. "Cool" even texture. Better at small sizes for captions. |
| **Bold sans-serif display** | Maximum visual mass. Architectural element. Highest weight-to-area ratio. |
| **Light/thin sans-serif display** | Tension through fragility at large scale. A whisper that's physically huge. |
| **Condensed type** | Increases density without increasing area. Compresses horizontal rhythm. |
| **Extended/wide type** | Fills horizontal space with fewer characters. Architectural beams and barriers. |
| **Typewriter/monospace** | Documentary authenticity, personal voice, raw/undesigned origin. At display scale, creates anti-display tension. As body text, creates uniform tonal equalizer (~45-55% grey). Reduces reading speed ~10-15% vs. proportional type — makes reading a more conscious act. Signals process/labor. |
| **Handwritten/script** | Disrupts mechanical systems. Introduces human energy. A different voice, not decoration. |
| **Irregular/hand-drawn face** | Prevents layouts from reading as systematic or corporate even when structure is disciplined. Sets anti-polished tonal range. |
| **Blackletter** | Scale-dependent signifier: at 3 scales = 3 functions (architectural framing, display headline, body texture). At body size, reads as dense dark texture. At display, reads as historical authority. At architectural scale, reads as pure structure. |
| **Extreme letterspacing** | Transforms reading matter into structural beam. Reduces typographic color. Creates permeable lattice. Forces character-level processing. |
| **Narrow column, tight leading** | Creates dark vertical mass — text as visual anchor/ballast, not primarily for reading. |

**The rule:** Choose typeface classification based on what structural job needs doing. The job dictates the tool.
